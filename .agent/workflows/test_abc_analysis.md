---
description: How to test the ABC Analysis feature
---

1. Login as a Manager.
2. Navigate to "Produtos" in the sidebar.
3. Click on the "Curva ABC" tab (next to "Lista").
4. Verify the ABC Analysis dashboard:
   - Check the Summary Cards (A, B, C, Sem Vendas).
   - Check the Revenue Distribution Chart.
   - Check the Detailed Product Table.
5. Test filters:
   - Change the period (30, 60, 90 days).
   - Use Custom dates.
6. Verify that products with no sales appear in the "Sem Vendas" class (if any).
